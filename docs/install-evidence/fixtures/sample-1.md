# Sample 1 — sandbox sourdough notes

Tested the 25% whole-wheat blend at 78% hydration over the weekend.
Crumb opened up better than the all-AP control. Three takeaways:

- Higher hydration on whole-wheat doses doesn't slacken the dough the
  way it does on AP; the bran absorbs aggressively in the first 30
  minutes of autolyse.
- Bulk fermentation cut by ~15% vs the AP baseline. The bran's enzyme
  load accelerates fermentation. Watch for over-proofing.
- Crust caramelization was darker at the same oven temp + time. The
  whole-wheat sugars participate in Maillard more than AP starch does.

Next: try 35% whole-wheat at 80% hydration. Document score-depth vs
oven spring on the same flour ratio.
