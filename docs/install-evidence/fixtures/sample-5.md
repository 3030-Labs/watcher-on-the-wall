# Sample 5 — gardening log 2026-05-15

First real planting weekend. Bed prep done last week; tomato + pepper
starts going in today, lettuce seeds direct-sown.

## Layout

- **Bed A** (full sun, south-facing): 4× cherry tomato starts, 2× San
  Marzano, 3× jalapeño. Trellis stakes at planting.
- **Bed B** (partial shade until 11am): 2× lettuce rows (oakleaf,
  little gem), 1× row of arugula. Re-seed every 3 weeks for
  succession.
- **Container row** (back patio): 1× basil, 1× thyme, 1× cilantro.
  Cilantro will bolt by July; plan a second pot in 6 weeks.

## Notes

- Soil pH tested at 6.4 across both beds — good range for Solanaceae.
  No amendments needed.
- Mulched with straw immediately after planting; reduces moisture loss
  + suppresses weeds.
- Drip irrigation set for 20 min/day, twice a day, until starts
  establish. Will drop to once a day after 2 weeks.

## Watch list

- Cherry tomato `Sun Gold` is the variety I've had best luck with;
  the others are experimental this year.
- Aphid pressure on peppers last year was high. Plan: companion-plant
  with basil + check undersides weekly. Ladybug release if early
  infestation.
